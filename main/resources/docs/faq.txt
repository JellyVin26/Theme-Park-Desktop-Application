How to book a ride: Go to 'Book Ride' in the app and select your preferred time.
Can I cancel my booking?: Yes, but only 24 hours before the scheduled time.
What are the park opening hours?: We are open daily from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
Do I need to book tickets in advance?: We recommend booking online to avoid queues.
Are there discounts for children?: Yes, children under 12 get a 50% discount.
Is outside food allowed in the park?: No, outside food and drinks are not allowed.
Do you have wheelchair accessibility?: Yes, the park is fully wheelchair accessible.
What happens if it rains?: Most rides operate in light rain, but some may close temporarily.
Can I upgrade my ticket to a season pass?: Yes, visit the ticket counter for upgrades.
Is parking available at the park?: Yes, there is ample parking for visitors.
